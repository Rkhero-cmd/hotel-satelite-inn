About Us

Your Home in the Heart of Satellite

Hotel Satellite Inn is Ahmedabad's trusted address for comfort and convenience. Located in the heart of Satellite-Prahlad Nagar at Devpriya Complex, just opposite Natraj Medical Store and steps from Titanium City Centre — we are at the crossroads of the city's most vibrant district.

With 42 well-appointed rooms, a 24-hour front desk, and warm hospitality, we cater to families, couples, and business travelers alike — all at genuinely affordable prices.
